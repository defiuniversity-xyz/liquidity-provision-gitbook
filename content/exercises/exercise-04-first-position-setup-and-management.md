**When to Withdraw**:
- [ ] IL consistently exceeds fees
- [ ] Better opportunities elsewhere
- [ ] Risk limits exceeded
- [ ] Need capital for other uses

**When to Rebalance**:
- [ ] Price moved significantly
- [ ] Out of range (V3)
- [ ] Better range available
- [ ] Gas costs justified

**When to Add More**:
- [ ] Position performing well
- [ ] Within risk limits
- [ ] Fees > IL consistently
- [ ] Have additional capital
